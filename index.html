Our innovation project is on the dangers of marine noise pollution.


Noise pollution is one of the ocean's issues that we feel isn't addressed as much as it should be. Thousand of animals every year are affected by noise pollution. The affects of noise pollution are deafness, fainting, and even worse, death.


The main causes are construction, steamships, and navy boats. These create intense noise emissions, which can cause severe damage to habitats.


This is quite sad and unfortunate, but luckily, there are some ways we can solve this issue.
